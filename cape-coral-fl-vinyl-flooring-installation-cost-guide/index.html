Local walkthrough reality

Cape Coral projects should be priced around the home, not only the square footage. Homes average about 25 years old, many are single-family, and humidity plus summer heat can affect acclimation, storage, subfloor preparation, transitions, and scheduling.

Project variables to compare

Subfloor condition, moisture control, furniture movement, continuous layout, trim, and transition details can move a vinyl flooring estimate. Standard replacement usually does not require a permit, but structural, electrical, or plumbing work can change that.
